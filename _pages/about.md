I am an undergraduate in the Top-Notch Undergraduate Training Program in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at East China Normal University (ECNU), Shanghai.

My engagement with mathematics has always been driven by independent discovery.These early experiences, pursued outside the standard curriculum and often against skepticism, solidified my belief that persistent inquiry is the only path to understanding. I am currently preparing for advanced study in algebraic number theory under the guidance of my mentor, working through Nancy Childress's Class Field Theory to build the foundation necessary for investigating the arithmetic of abelian extensions.Research Interests: Algebraic Number Theory, Class Field Theory, Algebraic Structures, L-functions.

I am based in Shanghai and welcome correspondence with anyone interested in similar arithmetic landscapes.
